📖 MARS SURVIVAL FIELD GUIDE

🌡️ THERMAL REGULATOR

Device maintaining suit temperature. Mars ranges from -125°C to 20°C. Failure = death within hours.

🌪️ DUST STORM

Planet-wide storms lasting months. Dust is toxic (perchlorates) and electrostatically charged.

☢️ RADIATION / CME

Mars lacks magnetic field. Solar flares (CMEs) deliver lethal radiation without shielding.

💨 CO₂ SCRUBBER

Removes CO₂ from air. Mars' atmosphere is 95% CO₂ — without it, you suffocate.

🧊 WATER RECYCLER

Reclaims moisture from breath/sweat. Without it, you need 3-5L water daily.

⚠️ MARTIAN GRAVITY

38% of Earth's. Causes muscle/bone loss. Movement feels different.

🕳️ LAVA TUBE

Natural underground tunnels formed by ancient lava flows. Provide natural radiation shielding and temperature stability.

🧲 MAGNETIC DEFLECTOR

Emergency shield that creates artificial magnetic field to deflect charged solar particles. Extremely power-hungry.

💊 ANTI-RADIATION MEDS

Pharmaceuticals containing potassium iodide and radioprotective compounds. Mitigates cellular damage from radiation exposure.

⛏️ REGOLITH

Loose Martian soil. Sharp, abrasive, contains toxic perchlorates. Damages equipment and skin.

🏜️ SUBSURFACE ICE

Water ice buried beneath Martian soil. Critical resource for drinking water and fuel production.

🔴 WHY MARS IS RED

Iron oxide — rust. Surface oxidized iron gives the red color.
